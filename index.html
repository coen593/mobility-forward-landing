Benefits of our Model

  • The model provides a framework for scalable and compliant coverage, intended to be globally accessible. This allows for a single-point policy framework across multiple jurisdictions, providing portable protection.

  • The model leverages the latest digital innovations through a technologically advanced administrative platform, which is scalable, reliable, and easily accessible via a web and mobile application.

  • Collective purchasing power will significantly reduce premiums for members. In the future, large employers can also save costs by potentially retaining risk internally and avoiding insurer overheads.

  • The model supports flexible, modular coverages that can adjust to members' evolving needs. It allows for the creation of tailor-made protection depending on the community's feedback.

  • Members can benefit from digital access to risk insights and claims data, and adjust the individual contributions according to their future.

  • The ultimate goal is to develop a community-driven Peer-to-Peer (P2P) protection model as an alternative to traditional insurance, offering flexible, transparent, and affordable coverage.
