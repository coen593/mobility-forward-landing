Benefits of our model

  • It provides a framework for scalable and compliant coverage, intended to be globally accessible. For expatriates, this allows for a single-point policy framework across multiple jurisdictions, providing "portable" protection.

  • The model integrates digital technologies through a technologically advanced administrative platform, which is scalable, reliable, and easily accessible via a mobile app and AI agent.

  • Collective purchasing power is expected to significantly reduce premiums for members. Large employers can also save costs by potentially retaining risk internally and avoiding insurer overheads.

  • The structure supports flexible, modular coverages that can adjust to members' evolving needs. It allows for the creation of tailor-made products, similar to a Captive Company structure.

  • Members can benefit from digital access to risk insights, and claims data.

  • The ultimate goal is to develop a community-driven Peer-to-Peer (P2P) protection model as an alternative to traditional insurance, offering flexible, transparent, and affordable coverage.
